# Victim Representation Minisite

## What this is
Single-page minisite proving that progressivism's moral authority depends on unauthorized, instrumental, serial representation of victims. Hebrew RTL with English blockquotes.

**Title**: "מי מינה אותך נציג?" (Who Appointed You as Representative?)
**Author**: Zvi Shalem / צבי שלם — Diagonal (אלכסון) project

## Structure
- Opening: The Distinction (victims are real; representation is false)
- Ch 1: Marx's Proletariat as Weapon
- Ch 2: The Failure (capitalism worked, workers stabilized)
- Ch 3: Migration to Race (Marcuse's reservoirs of misery)
- Ch 4: Migration to Gender (Butler's instruments of deconstruction)
- Ch 5: The Postmodern Turn (who defines knowledge)
- Ch 6: Serial Production (assembly line of victimhood)
- Ch 7: Gnostocracy (the mechanism exposed)
- Closing: The Antidote (specify, explain, justify)
